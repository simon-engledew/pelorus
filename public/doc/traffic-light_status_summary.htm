Traffic-light status summary

Comments and discussions

The status of a comment is set by the user submitting the comment.

The status of a discussion is the worst case of all comments since the last over-riding comment (including that over-riding comment).

Factors

The status of a factor is the worst case of its values status and its discussion status.

For a number or date factor:

At the basic level (single-valued target and predicted/actual) the values status is red if the predicted/actual is worse than the target, otherwise it is green.

At the advanced level (three-valued target and predicted/actual) the values status is calculated using the following rules in order:

•if worst is worse than fail, the status is red

•if likely is worse than report, the status is amber

•otherwise the status is green.

For a Yes/No factor, the values status is:

•green if the predicted/actual value matches the target value (i.e. both are Y or both are N)

•red if the predicted/actual value is the opposite of the target value (i.e. one is Y and one is N)

•amber if the predicted/actual value is ?.

Risks

The status of a risk is the worst case of its severity status and its discussion status.

For a basic-level risk, the severity status is set by the Risk Manager.

For an advanced-level risk, the severity status is governed by the adjusted severity:

•if the adjusted severity is ≥ 15, the status is red

•if the adjusted severity is between 5 and 14 (inclusive), the status is amber

•if the adjusted severity is <5, the status is green.

Goals

The local status of a goal is the worst case of its sub-goals status, factors status, risks status and discussion status.

The supported status of a goal is the worst case of the statuses of all supporting goals that are propagating their status.

The traffic-light status of a goal is the worst case of its local status and its supported status.

Map

The status of a map is the worst case of its goal statuses and its discussion status.
