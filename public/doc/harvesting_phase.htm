Harvesting Phase

Objective

To achieve the (head) goals in the map that represent the business benefits.

Actions

1.Continue to monitor the goals in the map—particularly the head goals that represent the business benefits—and to update factor predicted/actual values and risks as appropriate.

2.Whenever it is recognised that either

(i) there is a danger of the target benefits not being achieved, or

(ii) there is an opportunity to achieve further benefits

revisit actions from the Definition and Delivery phases as appropriate to overcome the danger or seize the opportunity.
